Community Science Museum

Semester Project 1

For this project I was tasked to make a website for an interactive local Community Science Museum opening in my city. Its core target audience was primary and middle school children (ages 7-15) and families with young children. This was my first full website build in one course at Noroff, and it was made over 4 weeks with designing and building the website with HTML and CSS.
