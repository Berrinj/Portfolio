Community Science Museum

Semester Project 1

For this project I was tasked to make a website for an interactive local Community Science Museum opening in my city. Its core target audience was primary and middle school children (ages 7-15) and families with young children. The museum wanted an informative, appealing website to attract middle school pupils and their parents. The website had to be informative and engaging, encouraging viewers to visit the museum.

This was my first full website build in one course at Noroff, and it was made over 4 weeks with designing and building the website with HTML and CSS.
